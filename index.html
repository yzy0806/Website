Gill Sans badge

Previous Next

       This project is about design badges using a typeface I like. My choice is Gill Sans.
       The first one I am trying to use positive/negative space using a black background and white letters in different size and orientation to create a casual feeling.
       The second one is a compass. I used “Q” in the center because the the little tail in “Q” is like a needle on the compass.
       Third one takes advantage of letter “g” becuase "g" is a very distinct letter in Gill Sans, so I cut the “g” in half and put the upper half at the bottom and the lower half on the top but people can still recognize the two “g”s in the button.

Expressive Typography

Previous Next

       This project is about creating a design of a word to express the meaning of that word. The word I have is deception. So I use 3D/shadow effect create a strong shadow that overlaps the word to create an illusion. I also purposefully miss the letter e so people can still recognize the word without realizing it is not spelled wrong, which convey the idea of deception.
